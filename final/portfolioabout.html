About Matt:

Matt Sears is an Illinois State University graduate with a Bachelor's Degree in Music and is currently working towards a Bachelor's for Music Production in the Creative Technologies program. Matt lives and works in the Chicagoland area and has most of his life. He has a passion for music and sound design and he hopes he can follow his passion in his future works.
